Our Menu

Check out our selection of delicious food!

Chili Cheeseburger Half & Half

Homemade chili over an all-beef patty with fries and our famous onion rings. Get your burger LMT style (lettuce, mayo, and tomato) and/or top it with Heinz.

BBQ

Slow-cooked for 10 hrs, then chopped or pulled and added to our made-from-scratch sauce. It’s a recipe that’ll make you tilt your head back and smile.

Onion Rings

Each onion that goes into our rings is hand-punched and double-dipped and floured to give them that classic, thick golden crust. Each batch is prepared daily, then refrigerated until a Cheeseburger half and half plate is ordered. Then it’s into fresh fry oil and out on a plate in front of a happy customer.

Iced Tea

Making our tea is a slow, sweet process. Orange pekoe black tea steeped in boiling water–no filter. The tea is then strained, combined with sugar, and boiled once more for a bold, even flavor.

Hot Dogs

Have one all the way with chili, mustard, and onions on a steamed bun. Our dogs are so good you’ll get a strange urge to name your son Frank.
